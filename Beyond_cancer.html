What is Beyond Cancer?

Beyond Cancer is a student led organization aimed to raise awareness of common and treatable cancers in our local community. This objective is done through Informative info graphics posted on social media and also by going to local schools to give presentations and do activities with the children. We also hold fund raisers through merch and food sale events to raise money and give to those who are unable to afford the cancer treatment they need.

My role

The organization started with just 6 of my friends has now (as of writing this) expanded to include Assumption college, Shrewsberry international school and Mater Dei school. I joined towards the end of the first year of operations. My role mainly consists of helping to prepare, set up and run events such as the food sale, and merch sale at school fairs.

Another opportunity I had was to go on location and give a presentation to a local school about skin cancer, a common and treatable cancer if recoignized early on. I was able to see our project actually take action and potentially make a difference. We also did activities with the children at the school so, hopefully, it was memorable for them.

In the end, after we graduated, we handed down the organization to the next generation of students who will hopefully carry on the project and enact meaningful contributions to the local community. During my time as a part of the team, I really learned to work under pressure much better from selling my time selling various goods. I also learned to communicate much more effectly as a part of talking with various customers, children and team members.
