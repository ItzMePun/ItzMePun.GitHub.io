# What is the Bamboo Project?

The Bamboo Project is a student led organization aimed at raising
awareness on recycling and how to be more environmentally friendly
is an easy way in our everyday lives. We try to educate the community
on how we could all become more environmentally friendly through our
social media platform and by partnering with local companies and organizations
to push our cause further.

I was one of the founding members of the Bamboo Project. My role in the
organization is to research into topics related to our cause and summarise
the information to be posted. On top of that I was also part of organizing
and running events such as food sales which were fund raisers.

# What did we achieve?

We initially planed to hold a canal clean up with the organization
Terracycle Thai Foundation. However, due to the insufficient number
of people we managed to recruit and the hurdle of paperwork related
to the schools health and safety regualtions for field trips, the plan
sadly did not come to fruition.

Despite that failure, we shifted our aim with the funds we had to partner
with Beyond Cancer. Together we sourced and provided care packages for children
with cancer that included everyday objects made from recycled plastic. This was
given to the children alongside financial help towards their treatment.

# Conclusion

Through the two years of the project, despite many setbacks that
led to us not being able to fulfill our original goal to the degree
we had hoped, I feel that the impact we did make was at least a positive
one and in the direction we aimed for. Through this project, I have learnt
many skills such as to research and summarize a topic as well as t how to
contact other organizations formally. I really am grateful for the opportunities
we did have to do this.
Moving into the future, we decided to hand down the project to the next generation. 
We aim to have more realistic short term goals that could be built upon 
by later years. I hope that some day, it will become the organization we
all initially envisioned.
